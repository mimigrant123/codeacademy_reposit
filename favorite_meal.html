My favorite meal

My favorite meal is hotpot with Chinese cabbage, beef and oyster mushrooms.

    There is a recipy:
  • Cut beef on thin slices;
  • Wash cabbage and separate leaves;
  • Put 1 cabbage leaf and put on it slices of beef on all surface of leaf;
  • Repeat that process 5 times;
  • Cut it into 2 peaces and put it into the pot;
  • Repeat steps 3-5 one more time;
    • Add spices:
    1. Black pepper;
    2. Granulated garlic;
    3. Sesame oil;
    4. Soy souce;
    5. MSG.
  • Boil for 30-40 minutes.
